About Max Edward

    Hi all, my name is Max Edward. I am currently majoring in Digital Making and Fabrication at UAT. The core of my interests include 3D Printing and Modeling. I am also an amateur digital photographer, motorcycle mechanic, and dabble in 35mm film.

    Why I chose this major over anything else is because I love to design and make things. Ever since I was a child, I liked to take things apart and find out what makes them tick. I was the biggest Lego kid on the block and loved to make my own Lego creations. I'm now working on taking my knowledge in 3D Printing and Modeling to the next step and hope to make a career out of it.
